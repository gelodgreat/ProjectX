The School of Education–Liberal Arts–Music–Social Work (SELAMS) brings together four pioneering programs to create a venue for students to pursue their unique calling in excellence, creativity, and passion.

The Education Program molds students to become excellent teachers, sympathetic counselors of the youth, and responsible leaders of the community. Our graduates have become outstanding school administrators, supervisors, and teachers in premier schools locally and worldwide.

The Liberal Arts Program hones students to be effective media communicators and educators, political scientists, and versatile professionals in the fields of art and culture.

The Music Program trains students to develop into skillful local, national, and international artists - opera singers, composers, conductors, and leaders in the music field.

The Social Work Program fosters student growth in becoming competent and skillful workers in public welfare agencies, corporate institutions, and communities here and abroad. SELAMS stands for Prominent school in the country Dynamic response to modern challenges Significant contribution to the progress of the professional fields

Mission/Vision

The School of ELAMS affirms CEU’s vision of, and commitment to quality education founded on wholesome values and geared towards intellectual development, technological advancement, and world-class competence in the fields of Education-Liberal Arts-Music-Social Work. The School will provide efficient, effective, and enlightened inculcation of knowledge, skills, and values in line with the specific objectives of its programs.
