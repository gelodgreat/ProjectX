Clinical Laboratory

- Located on the ground floor
- The CEU clinical laboratory serves not only the students but also the faculty and the non-teaching employees of the university with varied aspects of laboratory examinations like clinical chemistry, bacteriology, serology, clinical microscopy, parasitology, hematology, cytology, and drug test.


Radiology Diagnostic Department

- Located on the ground floor
- The CEU radiology diagnostic department offers general x-ray of the skull, extremities and whole spine.


Social Arts Laboratories

- Located at the 4th floor
- The social arts laboratories are designed to empower students to achieve their maximum potential by building confidence and self-esteem in their social skills.
