Archived Materials Development Unit

- Located at the ground floor
- This unit houses historic documents and records pertaining to Centro Escolar University.


Biological and Physical Sciences Laboratories

- Rooms where classes in the biological sciences (botany, zoology, microbiology, genetics, anatomy, and physiology) and the physical sciences (chemistry and physics including pharmacy professional subjects) are held.


Teaching Learning Technology Department (TLTD)

- Located at the 2nd floor
- TLTD offers instructional services to carry out all curricular programs.


Library

- Located at the 2nd floor extension
- The main library and the campus libraries are fully Wi-Fi capable and equipped with internet connections along with printing facilities.


University Museum

- Located at the 2nd floor extension
- The museum houses valuable university historical documents, early office and classroom furniture and fixtures, art works, photographs and other memorabilia of the university.
