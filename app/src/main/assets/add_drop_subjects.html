Change of Matriculation: Total Dropping and Partial Adding/Dropping of Subject/s



A student who is officially enrolled may be allowed to add or drop a subject based on the prescribed number of units in the curriculum for a semester.

  1. Proceed to the Dean of the School/College for recommendation on subjects to be added/dropped and fill out Adding/Dropping Form. If all subjects will be dropped, submit letter of parent/guardian indicating approval of the dropping (total dropping only).
  2. Submit the form to SRMD for approval.
  3. Go to Accounting Department for assessment of fees.
  4. Pay to the Cashier.
  5. Submit all documents to SRMD.

If a student withdraws enrollment and has no intention to continue studies at CEU, he/she shall undergo an exit interview at the GCD.
