SLIGHT OFFENSES

  • These are acts that a student should refrain from doing for the maintenance of peace and order, cleanliness and the desired teaching-learning atmosphere inside the campus.

    The following are slight offenses:


    • 1st Offense - Counseling
    • 2nd Offense - Warning
    • 3rd Offense - 1 class-day Suspension
    • 4th Offense - 3 class-day Suspension
    • 5th Offense - 5 class-day Suspension
    • 6th Offense - 7 class-day Suspension
    • 7th Offense - 9 class-day Suspension
    • 8th Offense - 1 semester Suspension and Probationary Status the following semester
    • 9th Offense - No Readmission

    1. When already inside the school premises, failure or refusal to wear the prescribed University or approved school/college uniform; entering the school premises without ID and not in proper uniform for the 6th time
    2. Wearing extremely loose or tattered pants or tight pants with the prescribed University uniform/shirt
    3. Wearing tight skirt, mini-shirt or above the knee capri/pedal-pushers pants
    4. Going to movie houses, night spots and similar public places while wearing the University uniform
    5. Failure or refusal to wear the official University lD or improperly wearing the official University ID within the University premises
    6. Entering the campus in civilian clothes without first securing the required approval for exemption in the case of married or working students
    7. For male students, wearing of long/unkempt hair, earrings and body piercings with accessories, make-up, colored nail polish, outlandish hair style, hair color, or accessories; or not observing proper grooming or school attire
    8. For female students, wearing of outlandish make-up, hair color, hair style, or accessories, body piercing with accessories, other than a pair of earrings; or not observing proper grooming or school attire
    9. Wearing of body-hugging or hanging school/college t-shirt.
    10. Wearing a cap or improper headgear inside the classroom, chapel, auditorium, library or offices
    11. Displaying or making tattoo visible
    12. Using vulgar, indecent, foul, obscene, profane or improper language
    13. Loitering, running, shouting, or making/creating unnecessary noise in the classroom, corridor or on campus
    14. Causing commotion of any kind during convocations, programs or other activities
    15. Not disposing of litter found within three feet away from him/her
    16. Staying inside the classroom or laboratory without permission
    17. Evading a person in authority to avoid apprehension
    18. Any other action/s analogous to the foregoing
