Resident Aliens



Pre-Enrollment Guidelines

  1. Submit requirements for verification at SRMD Foreign Students Section.
  2. Proceed to the Dean of the school/college you are applying to, for interview and recommendation.
  3. Follow appropriate procedure for enrollment.

    4. ADDITIONAL REQUIREMENT: I-Card


Non-Resident Aliens



Pre-Enrollment Procedure

  1. Submit requirements to SRMD Foreign Students Section for verification of records.
  2. Proceed to the Dean of school/college you are applying to, present English Proficiency Certification and undergo interview. If student is unable to present English Proficiency Certification, he/she shall take intensive English Lessons Part A.
  3. Get referral from Dean to the Guidance and Counseling Department for the entrance examination and to the English Department for the English Proficiency Test.
  4. Proceed to the Cash Department and pay the entrance and psychological examination fees. Those applying for the Doctor of Dentistry program, shall also pay the fee for the Qualifying Examination for Dentistry.
  5. Take entrance and psychological examinations (and Qualifying Examination for Dentistry, if applicable) on the specified dates. Proceed to the International Languages Department for English proficiency test and interview.
  6. Claim Application Form for Admission from GCD. Claim also the English proficiency test result.
  7. Return to the Dean the test results for recommendation of acceptance and proceed to the University Registrar for approval, acceptance and enrollment.


Admission Requirements

  1. Official transcript of records
  2. Certificate of Graduation
  3. Police Clearance
  4. Drug Test
  5. Photocopy of Passport
  6. Appropriate Visa
  7. Informed Consent Form

    8. NOTE: The first 3 requirements should be authenticated by the Philippine Consulate abroad.


Filipinos Who Studied Abroad



Pre-Enrollment Procedure

  1. Submit requirements to SRMD Foreign Students Section for verification of records.
  2. Proceed to the Dean of the school/college you are applying to, for interview and referral for the entrance examination.
  3. Follow appropriate procedure for enrollment.

    4. ADDITIONAL REQUIREMENT: Photocopy of Passport



Enrollment in the Doctor of Dental Medicine Program

  • All students entering the Doctor of Dental Medicine and Master of Science in Dentistry programs shall pass the Qualifying Examination for Dentistry.



Post-Enrollment Procedure


  • Proceed to the ID Area and present validated COM; follow steps in applying for I.D. card.
  • Return to the Dean's Office and have your Certificate of Matriculation stamped "Registered."



Other Policies on Enrollment


  1. You are officially enrolled only when:
    • Your Certificate of Matriculation (COM) has been machine validated at the Cash Department; and
    • Your Certificate of Matriculation (COM) stamped "Enrolled" has been turned over to the Document Final Submission Area.
  2. A fine will be imposed for late registration.
