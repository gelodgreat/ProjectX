Transferees



Pre-Enrollment Procedure

  1. Proceed to SRMD for screening of requirements and interview.
  2. Proceed to the Dean of the school/college for submission of requirements and interview.
  3. Go to the Cash Department for payment of fee for drug test.
  4. Take drug test at the Clinical Laboratory within the day of payment.
  5. Claim drug test result at the Dean's Office on the designated date.
  6. Accomplish Application Form for Admission.
  7. Pay entrance examination fee at the Cash Department. Those applying for the Doctor of Dentistry program shall also pay the fee for the Qualifying Examination for Dentistry.
  8. Proceed to GCD to get schedule of examination(s) and submit application form.
  9. Take the examination at the GCD Testing Center.
  10. Get accomplished Application Form from GCD.
  11. Return to the Dean's office to present results of the examination and for evaluation of professional subjects.
  12. Proceed to the offices of the Academic Department Heads concerned or his/her equivalent for evaluation of Core subjects/units.
  13. Return to the Dean's Office for final interview and approval of enrollment. If admitted, sign Student's Pledge.
  14. Follow enrollment procedure as New Students. Documents to be submitted to SRMD are:
    Application for Admission Form, letter seeking admission with parent's/ guardian's signature, transfer credentials, scholastic records, transferee's and parent's/guardian's waiver, drug test results, pictures, birth certificate (NSO), character testimonies, curriculum checklist, Adviser's Form, Routing Form, Informed Consent Form, and I-Card (for aliens).


Admission Requirements

  1. Transfer credentials
  2. Original scholastic record bearing school seal and signature of registrar
  3. Good character testimony from an authorized official of former school
  4. Student's letter seeking admission to CEU with parent's/guardian's signature
  5. NSO copy of the birth certificate (original and photocopy)
  6. Three (3) copies of latest picture (1/2" x 1/2")
  7. Transferee and Parent's/Guardian's Waiver
  8. Drug Test
  9. Informed Consent Form
  10. Psychological Test
  11. For applicants to the School of Dentistry: Certificate of Eligibility



Enrollment in the Doctor of Dental Medicine Program

  • All students entering the Doctor of Dental Medicine and Master of Science in Dentistry programs shall pass the Qualifying Examination for Dentistry.



Post-Enrollment Procedure


  • Proceed to the ID Area and present validated COM; follow steps in applying for I.D. card.
  • Return to the Dean's Office and have your Certificate of Matriculation stamped "Registered."



Other Policies on Enrollment


  1. You are officially enrolled only when:
    • Your Certificate of Matriculation (COM) has been machine validated at the Cash Department; and
    • Your Certificate of Matriculation (COM) stamped "Enrolled" has been turned over to the Document Final Submission Area.
  2. A fine will be imposed for late registration.
