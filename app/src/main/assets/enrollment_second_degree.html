Enrollment for a Second Degree



Pre-Enrollment Procedure

  1. Proceed to the Dean of School/College for initial interview.
  2. Go to the Cash Department for payment of fee for drug test.
  3. Take drug test at the Clinical Laboratory within the day of payment.
  4. Claim drug test result at the Dean�s Office on the designated date.
  5. Accomplish Application Form for Admission.
  6. Return to the Dean's Office to submit all requirements and for evaluation of professional subjects.
  7. Proceed to the offices of the Academic Department Heads concerned for evaluation of General Education subjects/units.
  8. Return to the Dean's Office for final interview and approval of enrollment. If admitted, sign Student's Pledge.
  9. Follow enrollment procedure as New Students. Documents to be submitted to SRMD are: Application for Admission Form, transfer credentials, transcript of records, transferee's and parent's/guardian's waiver, drug test results, pictures, birth certificate (NSO), character testimonies, curriculum checklist, Adviser�s Form, Routing Form, Informed Consent Form and I-Card (for aliens).


Admission Requirements

  1. Transfer credentials (for degree holders from other schools)
  2. Original transcript of records with Special Order number, as applicable
  3. Good character testimony from the authorized official of former school
  4. Three (3) copies of latest lD photo (1/2" x 1/2")
  5. NSO copy of the birth certificate (original and photocopy)
  6. Drug test result
  7. Transferee and Parent's/Guardian's Waiver
  8. Certificate of Eligibility for applicants to the School of Dentistry
  9. PRC ID for applicants to the Pharm. D. Program.
  10. Entrance evaluation of subjects taken to be done by the Dean/Academic Department Heads
  11. Informed Consent Form



Enrollment in the Doctor of Dental Medicine Program

  • All students entering the Doctor of Dental Medicine and Master of Science in Dentistry programs shall pass the Qualifying Examination for Dentistry.



Post-Enrollment Procedure


  • Return to the Dean's Office and have your Certificate of Matriculation stamped "Registered."



Other Policies on Enrollment


  1. You are officially enrolled only when:
    • Your Certificate of Matriculation (COM) has been machine validated at the Cash Department; and
    • Your Certificate of Matriculation (COM) stamped "Enrolled" has been turned over to the Document Final Submission Area.
  2. A fine will be imposed for late registration.
