Library

- Located at the 1st and 2nd floor
- The main library and the campus libraries are fully Wi-Fi capable and equipped with internet connections along with printing facilities.


Cybrary

- Located in the 2nd floor library
- The internet facilities at the Cybrary supplement the print and online resources of the library and are devoted to research and academic related activities only.


Music Studio

- Located at the 4th floor
- There is a music studio for percussion students have a music studio with special sound-proofing while another music studio is designed for guitar students.


Music room

- Located at the 4th floor
- A music room with music book for courses in piano, voice and instruments is conveniently located in an adjoining room.


Mass Communication Laboratory

- Located at the 3rd floor
- Broadcast majors are likewise trained in the techniques for radio and TV production and direction. They also study the principles of video-making for education, information, entertainment, promotion, and culture purpose.


Teaching Learning Technology Department (TLTD)

- Located at the 2nd floor
- TLTD offers instructional services to carry out all curricular programs.
