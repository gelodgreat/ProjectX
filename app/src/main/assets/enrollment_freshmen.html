New Freshmen



Pre-Enrollment Procedure

  1. Proceed to the Guidance and Counseling Department (GCD), for application for entrance examination.
  2. Submit accomplished Application for Admission Form and three (3) 1 1/2" x 1 1/2" ID photos to the GCD; get schedule of examination and room assignment.
  3. Proceed to the Cash Department and pay the entrance examination fee.
  4. Take entrance examination at the specified date and place of examination.
  5. While waiting for examination results, prepare additional requirements for admission:
    • 5.1 Original Form 138 / H.S. Report Card
    • 5.2 Good character testimony from the High School Principal
    • 5.3 NSO copy of birth certificate (original and photocopy)
  6. Claim test result from GCD and the accomplished application form for admission.
  7. Proceed to the Dean to present the requirements and for interview. If admitted, sign Student's Pledge.


Enrollment Procedure

  1. Proceed to School/College enrolment area and present admission requirements to the enrollment adviser.
  2. Get copy of the Routing Form, Adviser's Form and Curriculum checklist.
  3. Fill out Adviser's Form and have adviser indicate the subjects, section and schedule.
  4. Wait for computer generated Certificate of Matriculation (COM) which contains Student Number and assessment of fees.
  5. If you are entitled to a discount, present approved supporting document to the Accounting Department and request for reassessment of fees.
  6. Proceed to the Cash Department or any authorized bank for payment of fees.

    7. IMPORTANT: Slot reservation for a class is good for 24 hours only.

    NOTE: If payment will be done at any authorized bank, the COM shall be stamped "PAID" at the Accounting Department before submission to Student Records Management Department (SRMD).

  7. Proceed to the Health Services Department for medical, dental and/or other requirements of the Department.
  8. Proceed to the Uniform Section and present validated COM and get uniform.
  9. Proceed to the DOCUMENT FINAL SUBMISSION AREA to submit all enrollment documents including Application for Admission Form, Form 138, pictures, birth certificate (NSO), character testimonies, curriculum checklist, Adviser's Form, Routing Form, Informed Consent Form and I-Card (for aliens). Retain the student's copy of the Certificate of Matriculation.

    10. For FOREIGN STUDENTS: Submit all documents at SRMD Foreign Students Section.

    A LATE REGISTRATION FINE SHALL BE IMPOSED FOR REGISTRATION ONE (1) WEEK AFTER THE OPENING OF CLASSES.

Post-Enrollment Procedure

  1. Proceed to the ID Area and present validated COM; follow steps in applying for I.D. card.
  2. Return to the Dean's Office to have COM stamped �Registered.�



Other Policies on Enrollment


  1. You are officially enrolled only when:
    • Your Certificate of Matriculation (COM) has been machine validated at the Cash Department; and
    • Your Certificate of Matriculation (COM) stamped "Enrolled" has been turned over to the Document Final Submission Area.
  2. A fine will be imposed for late registration.
