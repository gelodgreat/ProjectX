Established in 1921, the School of Pharmacy is a leading institution in the field. CEU Pharmacists excel with their unique training and extensive experience in the essentials of Pharmacy, constantly proving themselves to be among the best in their profession.

Students are consistently trained to be globally competitive, professionally competent, legally qualified, morally upright and patient and population-oriented pharmacy practitioners. The Pharmacy program is enhanced with the latest in laboratory equipment and library resources. Pharmacy students build strong careers as supervisors, owners or managers of drugstores, company pharmacists, quality control pharmacists, laboratory instructors, lecturers, food and drug regulation officers, hospital pharmacists, clinical pharmacists, directors of hospital pharmacies and product managers of pharmaceutical firms.

Vision:

To become the top provider of professionally competent pharmacist.

Mission:

Guided and inspired by the University\'s maxim CIENCIA Y VIRTUD, the School of Pharmacy is committed to provide its students the necessary knowledge, skills, values and competencies, so they will be professionally competent, legally qualified, morally upright, patient and population-oriented, and globally competitive pharmacists.
