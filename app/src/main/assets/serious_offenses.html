SERIOUS OFFENSES

  • These are acts that cause serious damage to a student's honor and dignity as a person, as well as to the honor, name and property of the University.

First Category

  • Any of the following serious offenses shall be punishable with Exclusion (Immediate Dropping from the Roll and No Readmission in the following term/semester):

    1. Forging/intercalation, falsification of and tampering with academic or official records, receipts, permits or documents of any kind
    2. Obtaining, submitting and using fake school receipts, admission and related documents
    3. Deliberate submission of fraudulent/falsified reports, invoices/receipts to conceal shortages in accountable funds
    4. Taking periodic/final examination papers but not passing his/her test papers
    5. Robbery/stealing
    6. Committing serious acts of vandalism or destruction of property belonging to the University, student, faculty, or non-teaching personnel
    7. Serious fighting inside or outside the campus and/or acts causing public scandal, moral damage, physical injuries, serious harm to personal safety, damage to school property or any graver consequences
    8. Entering the University premises while under the influence of alcohol or dangerous drugs
    9. Involvement in the selling/buying/possession/use/distribution of alcoholic beverages in the University and within the immediate vicinity of the University or during University activities held outside the University
    10. Involvement in the selling/buying/possession/use/distribution of dangerous drugs like shabu, marijuana and other synthetic drugs, whether in the University or outside the University
    11. Possession and/or use within University premises of any kind of deadly weapons such as guns, explosives, knives, bladed/pointed instruments and similar weapons
    12. Use of tear gas and other similar chemicals for any purpose other than self-defense
    13. Membership in a fraternity or sorority, or any campus organization not recognized by the University
    14. Participation/involvement in hazing
    15. Participation/involvement in any subversive activity inside or outside the University
    16. lnstigating, leading or participating in concerted activities leading to the stoppage of classes and preventing or threatening students, faculty members or non-teaching personnel from discharging their duties or from attending classes
    17. Final conviction by a court of law in a criminal case
    18. Bribery or corruption of universityâ€™s teaching or non-teaching personnel or fellow students
    19. Embezzlement/malversation of University/school/college/department student organization or class funds
    20. Collecting/paying bets for any forms of gambling
    21. Bullying of fellow students, teachers and non-teaching personnel of the University, either physically or through social media
    22. Posing for lewd photos or videos for online publications and other publications, including magazines, newspapers and posters
    23. Serious moral faults, such as abortion, sexual relations with a married partner, and pimping, committed inside or outside the University and proved to be harmful to the best interests of the University and having adverse influence on fellow students
    24. Deliberate submission of forged academic records and documents for the purpose of admission causing the school to be misled as to enrolling the student
    25. Plagiarism and other acts of academic dishonesty in Thesis or Dissertation
    26. Any other action/s analogous to the foregoing

Second Category

  • Any of the following serious offenses shall be punishable with 10-day Suspension and No Readmission the following term/semester:

    1. Writing, circulating or causing to be written or circulated any article or publication detrimental to the image of the University
    2. Committing any obscene, indecent, or malicious act inside or outside the University
    3. Misleading or deceiving school authorities in relation to school requirements
    4. Any other action/s analogous to the foregoing

Thrid Category

  • The following serious offenses shall be punishable as follows

    • 1st Offense - 5.00 in the grading period (Prelims/Midterm/Finals)
    • 2nd Offense - 5.00 in the course
    • 3rd Offense - Exclusion (Immediately Dropped from the Roll and No Readmission the following term/semester)

    1. Cheating in any examination or test
    2. Plagiarism and other acts of academic dishonesty in academic essays, papers and reports required during the course of the semester
    3. Any other action/s analogous to the foregoing

Fourth Category

  • The following serious offense shall be punishable with 5-day suspension 1st offense and Exclusion (Immediately Dropped from the Roll and No Readmission the following term/semester) for the 2nd offense:

    • Filing of any malicious and baseless complaint against a teacher or non-teaching personnel
