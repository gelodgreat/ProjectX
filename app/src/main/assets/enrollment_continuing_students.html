Continuing Students



Enrollment Procedure

  1. Proceed to the enrollment adviser and get Grades Clearance/Adviser's Form and Routing Form. For graduating students, accomplish Information Sheet for Graduating Student Form and submit to the Dean's office.
  2. In the case of students with deficiencies, proceed to the SRMD for verification/clearance of deficiencies.
  3. Proceed to enrollment area for advising on subject/class schedule.
  4. Wait for computer generated Certificate of Matriculation (COM) which includes assessment of fees.
  5. If entitled to a discount, present approved supporting document to the Accounting Department and request for re-assessment of fees.
  6. Proceed to the Cash Department or any authorized bank for payment of fees.

    7. IMPORTANT: Slot reservation for a class is good for 24 hours only.

    NOTE: If payment will be done at any authorized bank, the COM shall be stamped "PAID" at the Accounting Department before submission to the Student Records Management Department (SRMD).

  7. Proceed to the Health Services Department for medical, dental and/or other requirements of the Department.
  8. Proceed to the Uniform Section, present validated COM and get uniform.
  9. Proceed to the DOCUMENT FINAL SUBMISSION AREA to submit all enrollment documents including Grades Clearance/Adviser's Form and Routing Form. Retain the student's copy of the Certificate of Matriculation stamped enrolled.

    10. For FOREIGN STUDENTS: Submit all documents at SRMD Foreign Students Section.


Post-Enrollment Procedure

  1. Proceed to School/College enrolment area and present admission requirements to the enrollment adviser.
  2. Get copy of the Routing Form, Adviser's Form and Curriculum checklist.
  3. Fill out Adviser's Form and have adviser indicate the subjects, section and schedule.
  4. Wait for computer generated Certificate of Matriculation (COM) which contains Student Number and assessment of fees.
  5. If you are entitled to a discount, present approved supporting document to the Accounting Department and request for reassessment of fees.
  6. Proceed to the Cash Department or any authorized bank for payment of fees.

    7. IMPORTANT: Slot reservation for a class is good for 24 hours only.

    NOTE: If payment will be done at any authorized bank, the COM shall be stamped "PAID" at the Accounting Department before submission to Student Records Management Department (SRMD).

  7. Proceed to the Health Services Department for medical, dental and/or other requirements of the Department.
  8. Proceed to the Uniform Section and present validated COM and get uniform.
